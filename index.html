What is Turnabout Climate?

Welcome to my game, Turnabout Choice! This game teaches you about Climate Change in a fun and interesting way. You are trying to save the Earth from Climate Change by catching heat waves that are heating the Earth. Collect 150 heat waves to save the world!

How to play?

To move your character, use your left and right arrow keys!

Press the spacebar to continue the dialouge. Left and right to select choice. Click on your choice during the riddle!
